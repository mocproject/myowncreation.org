MyOwnCreation.org

Sharing a world of creativity, one brick at a time.

What is a MOC?

MOC stands for My Own Creation. It is a widely used acronym throughout the LEGO™ community to describe a LEGO™ creation of your own design.

What is this project?

There are currently few dedicated platforms for LEGO™ creators to share, collaborate, and interact with other LEGO™ fans. The MyOwnCreation.org team has come together to remedy this issue and build a new platform for the community as a whole. Whether you are an Adult Fan of Lego (AFOL) or just starting out, we want to give you the chance to show the world your amazing creations!

Is there a platform that does this already?

Until recently MOCpages.com by Sean Kenney was the largest platform dedicated solely to letting LEGO™ fans share their own creations, outside of the official LEGO™ website. We want to offer the community a streamlined experience that will expand upon the mission that Sean set out to accomplish with MOCpages while offering a friendly and collaborative environment.

Where can I learn more?

As the project gets off the ground we will be trying to gauge interest from the community. Follow us on social media, sign up for updates via email, or reach out directly:

  • Follow us on Instagram: @MOCProject
  • Join our page on Facebook: TheMOCProject
  • Connect with us on Github: MOCProject

Can I help?

Yes! We want this to be a project built by the community for the community. The current team consists of two LEGO™ loving data nerds learning as we go and we are open to any help you're able to offer! We could currently use contributors with knowledge in the following areas:

  • Graphic design
  • User interface design (Vue.js)
  • Image processing/file management
  • Enthusiasm for LEGO™!

MyOwnCreation.org and the MOCProject are not affiliated with LEGO™ or The Lego Group.
